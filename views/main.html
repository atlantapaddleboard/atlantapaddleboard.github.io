Welcome

Hello, welcome to Fitness Phoenix's paddle boarding web page. With summer on they way, a great way to stay fit while having a good time is paddle boarding. Paddle boarding gives you a good excersise while relaxing and enjoying yourself. I support many locations around Georgia such as:

  • Fort Yargo *Parking fees may apply
  • Tribble Mill
  • Lake Lanier *Parking fees may apply
  • Keith Bridge Park
  • Lake Alatoona
  • Morgan Falls Overlook Park
  • Stone Mountain *parking fees may apply

Equipment such as paddle boards, leash, life jackets, and oars are provided for you to use.

NOTE: Some boards are equipped to convert into a kayak with an optional seat and extended paddle head if interested.

* Restrictions apply.

**Boarders are limited to a weight limit of 200lbs.


Paddle boarding is a family experience that everybody should join in on. Your children are welcome to come along with us.

In order to participate, children must

  • should be able to swim without a flotation device
  • are preferred to be at least 12 years of age. Exceptions may be permitted based on skill.
