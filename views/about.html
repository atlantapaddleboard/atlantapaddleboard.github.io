About Paddle Boarding

Paddle boarding is a great experience where you can relax while also getting a great arm workout! It's a fun experience to have with your family and it's also easy to learn. No matter where you go, there is always a fun adventure to be had when you are paddle boarding. At my lessons, I will teach beginners how to paddle board and have a good time.

About me

I am a happy husband, and a father of two. I am very passionate about fitness and overall health. This has inspired me to become a personal trainer and create Fitness Phoenix Recently, I had the most amazing experience paddle boarding with my wife and have enjoyed it ever since. I figured, why not share this experience with everybody during the summertime. I now look forward to meeting new people and enjoying teaching them how to paddle board with their family.
