About Paddle Boarding

Paddle boarding is a great experience where you can relax while also getting a great full body workout! It's a fun experience to have with your family and it's also easy to learn. No matter where you go, there is always a fun adventure to be had when you are paddle boarding. At my lessons, I will teach beginners how to paddle board and have a good time.

About me

I am a happy husband, and a father of two. I am very passionate about fitness and overall health. This has inspired me to become a Certified Personal Trainer and start Fitness Phoenix to include paddle boarding as it has become such a joy for our family.
